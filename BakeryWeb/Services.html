Allergen Information

Please inform us of any allergies or dietary requirements. Our bakery handles wheat, eggs, milk, nuts (including almonds, walnuts, and pecans), soy, and sesame seeds. While we take every precaution to prevent cross-contamination, we cannot guarantee that any product is completely free from allergens due to shared equipment and facilities.

Gluten-Free Products: Our gluten-free items are prepared in a dedicated area with separate equipment to minimize cross-contamination. However, they are produced in a facility that also processes wheat.

Vegan Options: Clearly marked vegan products contain no animal products or by-products. Please ask our staff for the complete list of vegan-friendly items available daily.
