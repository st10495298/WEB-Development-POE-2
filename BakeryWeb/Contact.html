Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer gluten-free options?

Yes! We have a dedicated gluten-free section with breads, cakes, pastries, and more. All gluten-free items are prepared in a separate area to minimize cross-contamination.

How far in advance should I order a custom cake?

We require a minimum of 48 hours for custom cakes. For wedding cakes or large orders, we recommend booking 2-4 weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery within 25km of Cape Town CBD. Delivery charges start from R 75 and vary based on distance and order size. Same-day delivery is available for an additional fee.

Are your ingredients organic?

We use organic ingredients wherever possible and are certified by the South African Organic Standards Authority. We source from local organic farms to ensure freshness and support our community.

Can I visit the bakery kitchen?

Due to health and safety regulations, we don't offer regular kitchen tours. However, we do have scheduled baking workshops where you can see our kitchen in action and learn our techniques!

Do you cater for allergies?

Absolutely! We can accommodate most allergies including nuts, dairy, eggs, and gluten. Please inform us of any allergies when placing your order so we can prepare safely.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards, debit cards, EFT, and mobile payments including SnapScan and Zapper. For custom orders, we require a 50% deposit.

Do you offer vegan options?

Yes! We have a growing selection of vegan cakes, pastries, and breads. Our vegan chocolate cake and blueberry muffins are customer favorites.
